Biography

Early Life

  1. Kalyan was born as Konidela Kalyan Babu on 2 September 1971 to Konidela Venkata Rao and Anjana Devi in Bapatla, Andhra Pradesh.
  2. He is the younger brother of Chiranjeevi and Nagendra Babu. He started using the name "Pawan" after a martial arts presentation he organized to demonstrate his training.
  3. He holds a black belt in karate.
  4. Kalyan made his acting debut in 1996 with Akkada Ammayi Ikkada Abbayi - Then following blockbusters include Badri,thammudu,kushi,Tholiprema,jalsa,gabbar singh,Atharintiki daredhi,vakeel sab , bheemla nayak many more in his film career.

Political career

  1. Kalyan started his political career in 2008 as the president of Yuvarajyam,the youth wing of Praja Rajyam Party which was started by his elder brother Chiranjeevi.
  2. Kalyan founded a political party named Jana Sena Party on 14 March 2014. He wrote a book titled Ism which is also the ideology of the Jana Sena party.
  3. He provoked the Uddanam kidney disease crisis by various protests and a hunger strike, which was later controlled by the Government of Andhra Pradesh by constructing dialysis centers and implementing various schemes for the village.
  4. The Jana Sena Party has contested 140 constituencies in the 2019 Andhra Pradesh Legislative Assembly elections. Kalyan has contested two constituencies – Gajuwaka and Bhimavaram.
  5. Unfortunately lost in 2019 election and aiming for the 2024 elections with full power in return.
